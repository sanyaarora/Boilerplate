Why this topic?

The search for extraterrestrial life is an interesting topic because it's really a search to answer the question, "are we
alone in the universe?" The universe is so incredibly vast and we've explored so much of it, and yet we haven't found any
other life. How is it that the Earth is so teeming with life, and no other planet we've explored is?
